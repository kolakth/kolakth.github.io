Tawai Central Management System .

Tawai


Tawai is one of my freelance project that I worked with a team of two people. I responsible for Product design (redesigned) and Front-End developer.

The website is developed using Vue js with Material Design Framework, and back-end is laravel (PHP) along with MySQL database which handle by my friends. Website is hosted on Windows Server 2012. It's took about one and a half month for Front-End to near complete and total of three months.

There are two main users category that will be using website.


1. For public use (in which people could login and check for registed health related products(อ.ย.) that being valid or not.)

2. For private use (For health agencies).

Health agencies will be asked to sign in first.

Sign in page are the second page that agencies will have to sign in. So, website know which sector they belong to and redirect to the right page and roles.

Not a member yet? Well, they can sign up right away.

Users will be redirect to a sign up page. Sign up process is easy, they just have to fill all necessary information to get started. so they could sign up and wait for web administrator approval.
