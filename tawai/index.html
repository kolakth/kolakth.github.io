TAWAI

Tawai is one of my freelance project that I worked with a team of two people. I responsible for Product design (redesigned) and Front-End developer.

The website is rapid developed using Vue js with Material Design Framework, and back-end is laravel (PHP) along with MySQL database which handle by my friends.

Website is hosted on Windows Server 2012. It's took about one and a half month for Front-End to near complete and total of three months.

Tawai are designed for two type of users

Public access

People could login and check for registed health related products(อ.ย.) that being valid or not.

Health agency

People from health sector such as pharmarcist, doctor, nurse, state governer, and health center to report illegal use of unregistered drug, new unknown illness, and other health related case.

 How can I come up with a logo?

It’s came to principle that major users are pharmarcist.

Tawai are mainly designed for pharmarcist to report illegal use of unregistered drug, new unknown illness. So, first I think of a pills and the eye to watch for illiegal act; then circle represent the pills and also eye with the brand name for the website.

White & Simple

I want it to be the most simple as much as possible. Homepage are mostly white and lot’s of space to reduce confussion at first sight of visiting website.

Users will need to sign in 

Users will need to sign in first by clicking the upper right corner. It’s will redirect to sign in page.

Not a member yet? Well, they can sign up right away.

Users will be redirect to a sign up page. Sign up process is easy, they just have to fill all necessary information to get started. so they could sign up and wait for web administrator approval.

After signed in, Ovarall Case dashboard

This page tell all about case progress for each task with updated status. Whenever they need any furthur actions related to case including following below:

 They could view or edit or delete submitted case.
 They could create new health related case.
 They could view status for each cases.

Management dashboard for pharmarcist

They can also see their handled case and check if case is really the problem
and later create a report for each districs.

Add new health issue case

They can create new case to report and furthur process such as false advertisement, unregistered product(unlawful), or newly discovered patient symthoms.
